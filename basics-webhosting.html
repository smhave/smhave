Web Hosting

To put your website onto the Internet for the world to see, you need to put it on a webhost. Either for a fee or for free, a hosting company will store your site on their server, and they may provide you with a domain name as well. Of course, it is possible to host your own site your self.

iPage looks to be a real good paid hosting site. Most of the other paid hosting sites I found, such as godaddy and SiteGround have different plans with different costs, associated with how many features you want. Looking at iPage, it looks like they only offer one plan, and the price is very competitive with the other sites as far as their middle and more advanced plans are concerned. Some things I like about iPage are they provide a free online store, PayPal registration, many different shopping carts, 24/7 network monitoring, 24/7 phone and live chat support, visitor and traffic statistics and reports, unlimited disk space and mySQL databases, a website builder and mobile site optimizer. Looking at user reviews there are a few very negative ones, and most of them are overwhelmingly positive.
