What are Websites?

Websites are typically web pages whose primary purpose is to convey information to the end user. There is little in the way of interaction with the user; maybe submitting an email to receive a monthly newsletter, or entering in a search. Often, websites will offer no interaction with the end user at all.

Sometimes businesses do not need web apps for their purposes, and informational websites are all that are necessary. Informational websites are a lot less complex than web apps, and are therefore much easier to create and set up.

If you are a start up company, and you find that in the future you need a web app but for now all you need is an informational website, it is good to bring in people who can create your web app early, so the transition from making your informational website into a web app is smooth when the time comes.
