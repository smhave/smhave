The Dark Web

The Internet that can be scoured by Google and other popular search engines is only the tip of the iceberg out there. The rest of the iceberg is known as The Deep Web, accessible with certain applications such as Tor. Part of the deep web is The Dark Web. The Dark Web is where a lot of illegal activity goes on freely, such as drug and arms trading, and illegal pornography. The anonymous nature of the dark web makes it difficult to trace the individuals using the dark web for illegal activities.

Ted’s talk on the dark web was interesting. I went in knowing just a little bit about the dark web. I recently took CIT 58F Ethical Hacking with Mr. Walker last semester and he talked a little about the dark net. He showed an image that made an impression on my mind. The image likened the Internet to an iceberg. The Internet we see is just the tip of the iceberg. Below the waters, as the entire rest of the giant iceberg, is the deep web. The lowest part of that, a very big portion of it is the dark web.

The vast majority of Ted’s talk on the dark web was about illegal drug trade. He also mentioned whistle blowers use the dark net and that there’s illegal pornography on the dark net. I’ve also heard that there is a lot of black market arms dealing on the dark web, and that you can even hire hitmen on the dark web, and am kind of disappointed Ted didn’t talk about those things at all.
